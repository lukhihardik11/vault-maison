## Unique Features

- **Act structure** — The homepage is organized as a theatrical performance with acts and scenes
- **Bodoni Moda headings** — High-contrast serif font creates dramatic typographic impact
- **Warm gold accent** — `#E0C097` warm gold on deep indigo-black creates cinematic warmth
- **Curtain call banner** — Animated banner that "opens" like theater curtains
- **Dramatic quotes** — Large typographic quotes with cinematic styling
- **Scene cards** — Products presented as scenes in a performance
- **Act counter** — Visual counter showing which "act" of the shopping experience you're in
- **Program cards** — Category cards styled as theater programs
- **Spotlight effect** — Products illuminated by a virtual spotlight
