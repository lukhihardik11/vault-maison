# Clone Reference — Exact Measurements from Target Sites

## 1. CELINE.COM (PRIMARY REFERENCE)

### Navigation
- Layout: LEFT SIDEBAR (not top bar!) — ~260px wide
- Logo: "CELINE" top-left, uppercase serif, ~30px, black, bold
- Announcement bar: top center "SHOP NEW CELINE LIP BALMS | FREE SHIPPING & RETURNS" ~11px uppercase
- Nav links: stacked vertically in left sidebar
  - "CELINE NEW" / "CELINE SHOWS" — uppercase, ~11px, tracking wide
  - "CELINE SHOP WOMEN" / "CELINE SHOP MEN" / "CELINE GIFTS" etc
  - Font: ~11px uppercase, weight 400, letter-spacing ~0.15em
  - Line-height between links: ~20px
  - Groups separated by ~20px gap
- Bottom of sidebar: "STORE LOCATOR" / "SIGN IN / REGISTER" / "SEARCH"
- Close button (X) top-right corner

### Hero
- Full-viewport hero: dark/black video/image background
- "CELINE" centered in white, ~120px font size
- Hero takes ~100vh minus announcement bar
- Below hero: editorial campaign images

### Below Hero — Editorial Sections
- Section title: "ÉTÉ CELINE" — bold uppercase serif, ~36px
- Subtitle: "ETE CELINE COLLECTION / PHOTOGRAPHED BY ZOE GHERTNER" — ~11px uppercase
- CTA link: "DISCOVER THE CAMPAIGN" — ~11px uppercase, underlined
- Below: 2-column editorial images (50/50 split), edge-to-edge within content area
- Images: very large, ~600px tall, no gap between them (or minimal ~4px)
- Pattern: Title text → 2-col images → Title text → 2-col images (repeating)
- Left sidebar stays fixed while content scrolls

### Overall
- Background: white page, dark hero video
- Colors: Black (#000) + White (#FFF) only
- Very editorial/fashion magazine feel — NOT a product grid homepage
- Content area: right of sidebar, ~calc(100% - 260px)
- Homepage is editorial campaign imagery, NOT product listings
- No product cards on homepage — it's all editorial/campaign content
- Typography: mix of bold serif (titles) and light sans-serif (body)


## 2. MEJURI.COM (Category/Product Grid Reference)

### Top Navigation
- Announcement bar: ~32px height, black bg, white text, ~11px
- Main nav: ~56px height, white bg
- Logo: "MEJURI" left, sans-serif, ~20px bold
- Nav links: ALL JEWELRY | GIFTS | NEW IN | COLLECTIONS | GET INSPIRED
- Right side: SEARCH | STORE | Account | Wishlist | Bag
- Font: ~12px uppercase, sans-serif, weight 500

### Category Page Structure
- Breadcrumb: "Shop All" link, ~11px
- Page title: "RINGS" — large serif, ~48px, centered
- Sub-category horizontal scroll with circular thumbnail images + labels
- Filter bar: MATERIAL | STONE | ENGRAVABLE | % OFF | ALL FILTERS — left
- Product count "(215 Products)" — right, SORT dropdown far right

### Product Grid
- 4 columns desktop
- Cards: 1:1 aspect ratio image on light gray bg
- Badges: "NEW" / "ONLY A FEW LEFT" / "BACK IN STOCK" — top-left, ~10px uppercase
- Product name: ~13px, uppercase, weight 500
- Price: ~13px, weight 400; sale shows strikethrough + sale price + "30% OFF"
- Material: ~11px, gray, below price
- "ADD +" button at bottom of card, ~11px uppercase
- Gap between cards: ~16px
- Padding: ~24px horizontal


### Product Detail Page (PDP)
- Layout: 2-column, ~55% image left / ~45% info right
- Image: large square, light gray bg, product centered, ~700px
- Thumbnail dots/pagination at bottom of image area
- Right column:
  - Breadcrumb: "Shop All / Ring" — ~11px
  - Product name: "AURORA RADIANT ETERNITY RING" — ~18px uppercase, weight 500
  - Rating: "4.6 ★" — top-right
  - Price: original strikethrough + sale price + "30% OFF" — ~16px
  - Material swatch: small circle + label "18k Gold Vermeil, Lab Grown White Sapphire"
  - Size selector: horizontal number buttons (4-10) + "SIZE GUIDE" link
  - "ADD TO BAG" button: full-width, black bg, white text, ~48px height, ~13px uppercase
  - Wishlist heart icon: right of ADD TO BAG
  - Afterpay/Klarna: "From $15/month" — ~11px
  - Description: paragraph text, ~15px, weight 400, line-height ~1.6
  - Feature icons: Hypoallergenic, 18K Gold Plating, Recycled Silver, Eco-friendly
  - Accordion sections: MATERIALS | SPECIFICATIONS | SUSTAINABILITY | SHIPPING
