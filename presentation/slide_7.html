Strategic Value of Framework Diversity

Framework diversity is not an academic exercise — it serves three strategic purposes:
01
Proof of Concept Depth
Each concept is architecturally viable, not just a visual mockup. The framework choice proves the interaction pattern can actually be built and scaled in production.
02
Risk Mitigation
If one framework falls out of favor or has a critical vulnerability, 9 other proven alternatives exist within the Vault-Maison playbook, ensuring long-term resilience.
03
Team Flexibility
Different engineering teams have different strengths. A Vue-native team can build The Observatory (Nuxt.js). A React-native team can build The Vault (Next.js).
The technology layer is the foundation that makes each concept's signature interaction possible — from The Vault's biometric reveal (Framer Motion) to The Immersive Theater's WebGL constellation (React Three Fiber).
