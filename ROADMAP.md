# Vault Maison Roadmap

## Phase 1: Myth Creation and Private Access (Months 1–6)

The first phase focuses on building brand mystique without active selling. The primary objectives include recruiting 100 founding clients, establishing key industry relationships, and creating a robust content foundation. The team will operate with 5–7 people maximum, concentrating on brand identity development, digital presence foundation, industry seeding through press and influencer outreach, content creation, community building, and private preview events.

## Phase 2: Invitation Commerce (Months 7–18)

The second phase transitions to revenue generation through an invitation-only commerce model. Target revenue is $2–5M from founding clients, with a focus on refining product-market fit and building operational excellence. The product rollout begins with a signature collection of 15–20 pieces, followed by bespoke service introduction, seasonal limited drops, and customer expansion through referral programs. The team expands to 10–15 people.

## Phase 3: Scale with Integrity (Months 19–36)

The third phase targets $20–50M in annual revenue while maintaining luxury positioning during growth. Geographic expansion into priority markets, product line extensions, partnership development with luxury retailers and cultural institutions, and service enhancement form the core strategy. The team scales to 25–40 people with mature technology platforms and global logistics capabilities.

## Current Status

**Active Phase:** Research and Strategy Development (Pre-Phase 1)

The repository is currently in the foundational research and strategy development stage, producing the comprehensive market intelligence required to inform all subsequent phases.
